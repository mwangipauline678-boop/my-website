❓ FAQ & Help Center

📖 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my admission number? ▼
Your admission number is printed on your student ID card and on all official school correspondence. If you've lost it, please contact the registrar's office.
How is the mean grade calculated? ▼
The mean grade is calculated by averaging your scores in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. The average is then mapped to a grade: 80–100 → 90, 70–79 → 80, 60–69 → 70, 40–59 → 60, and 0–39 → 50.
I forgot my portal password. What should I do? ▼
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via your registered email. If you still have issues, visit the IT help desk.
Where can I find the class timetable? ▼
The up-to-date timetable is available on the student portal under "My Schedule". You can also check the notice board outside the administration block.
How do I pay my school fees? ▼
Fees can be paid via bank transfer, mobile money, or directly at the school's finance office. Please use your admission number as the reference for all payments.
What are the library borrowing rules? ▼
Students may borrow up to 3 books at a time for a period of 14 days. Renewals are allowed once if the book is not reserved. Late returns incur a fine of 50 KES per day.
