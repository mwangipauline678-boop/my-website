CHAPTER ONE

INTRODUCTION

1.1 Background of the Study

Technology has transformed the way businesses operate in the modern world. Organizations are increasingly adopting computerized systems to improve efficiency, accuracy, and service delivery. Online booking systems allow customers to reserve services through digital platforms without physically visiting business premises.

This project focuses on developing an online booking system that simplifies reservation processes and improves customer experience.

1.2 Problem Statement

Manual booking systems are slow, prone to errors, and difficult to manage. Customers may experience delays and double bookings due to poor record keeping.

1.3 Objectives

  • To develop an online booking system.
  • To improve booking efficiency.
  • To reduce manual errors.
